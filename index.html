About Me

Prologue

I am Vedant Joshi, currently enrolled in the M.S (Pharm) program in Pharmaceutics at the esteemed National Institute of Pharmaceutical Education & Research in Kolkata, which holds a commendable NIRF ranking of 33. Prior to this, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy from Savitribai Phule Pune University, a highly regarded institution that ranks at 12th position in the NIRF rankings.


My research pursuits in the field of Pharmaceutics have been focused on the fabrication and characterization of 3D printed biodegradable microneedles, a highly specialized area of study that requires extensive knowledge and expertise. Overall, my academic and research background, coupled with my experience in specialized areas of Pharmaceutics, has equipped me with a strong foundation and skills to excel in this field.
